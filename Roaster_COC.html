BITP Esports | Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans is a free-to-play mobile strategy video game developed and published by Finnish game developer Supercell. The game was released for iOS platforms on August 2, 2012, and on Google Play for Android on October 7, 2013. The game is set in a fantasy-themed persistent world where the player is a chief of a village. Clash of Clans tasks players to build their own village using the resources gained from attacking other player's villages; earning rewards, buying them with medals or by producing them at their own village. To attack, players train different kinds of troops using resources. The main resources are gold, elixir and dark elixir. Players can conjoin to create clans, groups of up to fifty people, who can then participate in Clan Wars together, donate and receive troops, and chat with each other. With its increasing influence on the competetive scenario of esports worldwide, we found it perfectly fitting to get step in the current with our own Team!. Our current team consists of 9 active playing players. The Esports Wing is an expressive and dynamic arena for both professionals and novices seeking to up their game and hone their gaming skills to excellence.
