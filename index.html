About Club

The Esports Wing of BITP Technical

Technical Club proudly invites you to: “The Esports Wing”, Bihar’s maiden venture into the cosmic craze of electronic sports and gaming. As the 3rd decade of the 21st century marks its transition, esports have never been more popular. With the architype inclining towards electronic platforms, esports were worth more than $1 billion in 2019! It doesn’t come as a surprise that the industry is expected to proliferate four-fold in the upcoming 7 years!

The Esports Wing strives to harness raw gaming potential and polish it to sheer perfection. With targeted training and collaborative representation, this wing of the Technical Club strives for global recognition in popular games like Valorant, Counter-Strike, Clash Royale, Clash of Clans, Battlegrounds Mobile India, COD(Mobile) and many more!

The Esports Wing is an expressive and dynamic arena for both professionals and novices seeking to up their game and hone their gaming skills to excellence. We are dedicated to weaving an amicable milieu promoting universal cooperation and fostering solidarity and positivity. The Esports Wing is confident that with a little direction to crude talent, our incredibly adept Rosters could win laurels on national and international levels also. We envision a facilitated community ingrained on the pillars of camaraderie, constructivism and sportsmanship. But above all, we believe in enjoying the sport and experiencing the thrill of every step of the game. So, what is the gamer within you waiting for? Turn on your screens and get ready to bring your a-game!
