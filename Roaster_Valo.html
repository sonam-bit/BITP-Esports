Valorant is a free-to-play first-person hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Microsoft Windows.

With its increasing influence on the competetive scenario of esports worldwide, we found it perfectly fitting to get step in the current with our own Team!. We are dedicated to weaving an amicable milieu promoting universal cooperation and fostering solidarity and positivity.
